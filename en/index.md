# Welcome to Marethar

This is the second edition of **Marethar**: a living world, contradictory, and ready to collapse the moment you begin to play in it.

Compared to the first edition, every section has been reconceived to offer more reactive material — less lore to memorize, more tensions waiting to detonate. The cultures are more faceted, the religions more dangerous, the maps more uncertain.

There are no official truths, no meta-plots to follow, no canonical characters to save. There are ruins that watch you, cults that judge you, and cities that change while you speak.

**Marethar does not wait. Enter — and begin to live it.**
