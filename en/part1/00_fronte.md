# 🜍 Part I – The World of Marethar

*Wind, voices, foundations unshared*

> *"To understand Marethar, one must first choose who one pretends to be while listening to it."*

This is not a unified world, but a fragile convergence. Every people speaks its own truth, every cult measures the sky with different instruments. Here gather beliefs and structures, maps of power, fragments of what is handed down — or imposed. Expect no coherence. But if you find contradictions, it means you have found the heart.
