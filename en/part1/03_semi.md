## 🝰 Adventure Seeds

*Twelve starting points for throwing the world into crisis*

> *"Every campaign begins when someone dares to ask: what would have happened if…?"*

### **The Lion's Oath**

**Location**: Desert of Qadmath
**Context**: The appointment of a new chieftain of one of the central Aridonian tribes is imminent. The most favored candidate is born of an honored lineage.
**Rupture**: A rumor spreads: the young man carries Rashian blood. Some wish to halt the appointment; others see in him a bridge toward peace.

**How to Begin Immediately**:

* A PC is a member of the tribal council and must vote.
* The PCs are merchants seeking alliances in the desert, but become entangled in the ritual.
* Someone pays the PCs to find proof of the truth — or destroy it.
  **Tags**: identity, blood vs. culture, local intrigue, ritual vote

### **The Map of the Broken Line**

**Location**: Central Marethar, Lysandrian sea lanes
**Context**: A navigator dies, leaving behind an incomplete map. It indicates a maritime route that "should not exist" — through submerged reefs and islands absent from all official charts.
**Rupture**: Other merchants want the map, the Naval Guild considers it dangerous, and certain priests claim the route "is cursed."
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are hired as crew for the expedition.
* The map is stolen and a PC is suspected of having it.
* A rumor claims the route leads to a port "before time."
  **Tags**: exploration, sunken myth, commercial rivalry, sea and memory

### **Fire on the Forge**

**Location**: Kingdom of Vallan, mining regions
**Context**: A new metal is discovered: light, resonant, difficult to smelt. The most experienced artisans speak of "answers in the alloy."
**Rupture**: The Artisans' Brotherhood bans the working of the metal. Some call it "soul-metal"; others call it "impure metal."
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* A PC is an apprentice and receives a fragment.
* A noble commissions a secret weapon against the guild's veto.
* A rumor claims that blades forged from this metal speak.
  **Tags**: technology and superstition, guilds, latent power, living matter

### **The Grain Treaty**

**Location**: Republic of Premia / Diarchy of Ashania
**Context**: The cultivated plains of Preiacoria are struck by famine. The harvest has been lost and the central granary burned under ambiguous circumstances.
**Rupture**: Ashania offers to send food stores, but only in exchange for the right to build a temple of the solar cult in the heart of the republic.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are dispatched by the Senate to negotiate terms with the diarchs.
* One character is a devotee of the Ashanian cult, but keeps it secret.
* An extremist faction in Premia wants to sabotage the accord before it is signed.
  **Tags**: politics, religion, diplomacy, hunger and faith

### **The Light on the Lake**

**Location**: Rhithian League, northern islands
**Context**: Each evening, for a few moments, a body of water shines with an iridescent light that no one can explain.
**Rupture**: It is said to be the cyclical manifestation of a sunken city. The Naval Guild wants to forbid access to the lake on "grounds of safety."
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are cartographers tasked with surveying the phenomenon.
* A relative of one of the PCs vanished in the lake ten years ago.
* A minor cult holds that the light is a portal and is preparing to "follow the call."
  **Tags**: mystery, folklore, water, ancient awakening

### **The Silence of Shahin**

**Location**: Aridonia
**Context**: The Tree Sect — one of the oldest cults — has abruptly ceased every public rite, every word, every song.
**Rupture**: The priests remain silent. It is said that Shahin has fallen silent to punish a collective sin… or perhaps to reveal something.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are sent by a chieftain to demand explanation.
* A PC is a former devotee of the cult and receives troubling dreams.
* Another cult seeks to exploit the silence to assert dominance.
  **Tags**: mysticism, silence, heresy, fractured faith

### **The Serpent and the Wheel**

**Location**: Rashian Empire
**Context**: In the poor quarters, a clandestine cult spreads that venerates the symbol of a serpent coiled around a wheel.
**Rupture**: That symbol reappears on ancient tombs and in the dreams of many. The nobles wish to suppress the cult, but some are beginning to wear its mark.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are infiltrated into the cult on orders from the military.
* One of them was born with a birthmark that reproduces the symbol.
* A prominent noble publicly adopts the cult.
  **Tags**: cult, identity, symbolism, revolution

### **The Oath of Linia**

**Location**: Empire of Linia
**Context**: A senator proposes the abolition of noble privileges.
**Rupture**: Three of his children vanish the same night. The nobles deny any involvement… but the symbols left at the scene indicate a ritual vengeance.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs must find the children — or prevent their return.
* A PC is one of the children, who fled by design.
* A secret amendment could change everything.
  **Tags**: politics, disappearance, reform, power

### **The Water Oath**

**Location**: Empire of Doria / Naharim
**Context**: The Dorian River has changed course, devastating fields and sacred temples.
**Rupture**: Some say it is divine punishment. Others suspect engineering sabotage. Meanwhile, a tributary begins to sing.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs must mediate a dispute between two communities.
* A PC has troubling dreams tied to the river.
* A river cult sees the event as fulfilled prophecy.
  **Tags**: water, divinity, living nature, disaster

### **The Salt Fever**

**Location**: Hamania
**Context**: The salt workers begin to suffer a delirious fever.
**Rupture**: The afflicted tell of a creature made of salt that walks among the dunes and calls them by name.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are healers or physicians sent by the Grand Duke.
* A fever-vision foreshadows an imminent disaster.
* The authorities want to forcibly shut down the salt flats.
  **Tags**: disease, visions, industrial, body and myth

### **The Sixth Gate of Allasia**

**Location**: City-State of Allasia
**Context**: After an earthquake, an ancient gate bricked up for centuries reopens in the city walls.
**Rupture**: No one remembers its origin. Some say it "leads nowhere, but changes those who pass through it."
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs are tasked with exploring it.
* A PC receives mysterious letters from "one who has already crossed."
* The authorities want to seal it. But the city has already begun to change.
  **Tags**: metaphysical, city, ruin, threshold

### **Copper and Sand**

**Location**: Desert of Qadmath, Aridonia
**Context**: A caravan vanishes. Weeks later, a guard returns — covered in molten copper, mute.
**Rupture**: The copper appears alive. The sand around her no longer holds footprints.
**How to Begin Immediately**:

* The PCs must reconstruct the caravan's route.
* A cult proclaims that the woman has been "purified."
* The copper spreads. And it changes those who touch it.
  **Tags**: strange, desert, transformation, loss
