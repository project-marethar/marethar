# 🜄 Part II – Atlas of Marethar

*Uncertain cartographies, contested chronologies*

> *"Cities have official names, and then names used only by those who live in them."*

Some have tried to measure Marethar: with maps, dates, symbols. Some have failed, but left ink and dust as a guide. This atlas gathers cultures, states, genealogies. Some are still alive; others survive in proverbs. Some lie. Nearly all have tried to kill the others at least once.
