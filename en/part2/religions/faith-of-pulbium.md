# Faith of Pulbium

**Type:** Organized
**Form:** Shamanism
**Culture:** Aurelian
**Area:** Aurelia
**Faithful:** 24,860

**Description:** The Faith of Pulbium venerates Pulbium, the Ancient. It is an organized religion that includes shamanic rituals and ancestor veneration. The faithful believe that Pulbium offers protection and wisdom, guiding the community through difficult times.

**Origins and Religious Practices:** The faith originates in the rural Aurelian communities, where Pulbium is regarded as the guardian of ancient wisdom. Rituals include collective meditations, offerings of grain and wine, and purification ceremonies during the harvest seasons.

**Clergy Structure:** The Faith of Pulbium has an organized clergy of priests who administer rituals and guide communities. The priests are trained in knowledge of medicinal herbs and spiritual care.
