# Faith of Tudeshkhast

**Type:** Organized
**Form:** Dualism
**Culture:** Orethian
**Area:** Orethia
**Faithful:** 6,012,780

**Description:** The Faith of Tudeshkhast is grounded in dualism and venerates Tudeshkhast, the Flying Sage of Destiny. This religion organizes complex rites celebrating the balance between light and darkness, life and death. The faithful believe that Tudeshkhast governs the fates of all, and that equilibrium is essential for prosperity and peace.

**Origins and Religious Practices:** The Faith of Tudeshkhast has ancient roots in the mountains of Orethia, where communities sought to understand and balance the forces of nature. Rituals include offerings of food and drink, meditations, and purification ceremonies. The principal celebrations take place at the seasonal transitions, which symbolize the natural balance.

**Clergy Structure:** The religion is guided by an organized clergy of priests and sages who study the sacred texts and lead the faithful through the rituals. The priests are also responsible for preserving ancient knowledge and transmitting traditions.
