# 🜃 Part III – Beyond the Visible

*Fractures, beliefs, what slips away*

> *"The only difference between myth and news is the time it takes to forget them."*

Beneath the official chronicles, in the folds of songs and ruins, Marethar also tells what it should not remember. Here lie the creatures that were dreamed, the relics that speak in silence, the documents without an author. Everything that disturbs. Everything that keeps returning.
