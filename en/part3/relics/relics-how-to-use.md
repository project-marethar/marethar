# How to Use Them in Play

These objects are not merely **tools with bonuses**. They are **narrative catalysts**.

> **Narrative use ideas**:

* Objects that activate only under emotional, moral, or historical conditions
* Relics that change form or effect depending on the bearer's culture
* "Useless" objects that are nonetheless central to a vision, a dream, or a revelation
* False artifacts, created to manipulate entire communities
