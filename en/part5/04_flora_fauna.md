## 🜁 Flora and Fauna of Marethar

The Marethar region is a place of extraordinary biodiversity, with a variety of ecosystems supporting a wide range of plants and animals. From dense forests to open grasslands, from mountains to marshes, each environment harbors unique species that have adapted their lives to the specific conditions of their habitat. Marethar is also populated by mythic creatures which, inspired by the respective mythologies of the region's cultures, add a further layer of wonder and mystery.

### Flora

#### Forests of Linia

The forests of Linia, situated within the Empire of Linia, are characterized by dense and varied vegetation.

**Principal Plants:**

* **Linian Cedar:** A towering tree with resistant and aromatic wood, used in construction and craftsmanship.
* **Golden Fern:** A rare and precious fern, known for its medicinal and magical properties.
* **Khazar Climbing Vine:** A climbing plant bearing sweet and nutritious berries, often harvested to produce fine wines.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Forest Dragon:** A winged reptilian creature said to protect the forests from loggers. Inspired by dragons of European mythology.

#### Plains of Vallan

The plains of Vallan, situated within the Kingdom of Vallan, are famous for their fertility and abundance of cultivated plants.

**Principal Plants:**

* **Vallanian Wheat:** A hardy and highly productive wheat variety, the foundation of the local diet.
* **Vallanian Sunflower:** Cultivated both for its nutritious seeds and for sunflower oil.
* **Blue Flax:** A plant used to produce high-quality textiles, highly valued in trade.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Harvest Spirits:** Benevolent entities believed to protect the crops and guarantee abundance. Inspired by the agricultural spirits of European cultures.

#### Mountains of Orethia

The mountains of Orethia are rich in mineral resources and host flora adapted to the mountain climate.

**Principal Plants:**

* **Black Pine:** A hardy tree used for building fortifications and weapons.
* **Wild Rhododendron:** A plant with vivid flowers, used in traditional medicine.
* **Iron Moss:** A rare plant that grows near iron mines, used to strengthen metal alloys.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Mountain Griffins:** Creatures with the body of a lion and the head of an eagle, symbols of strength and protection. Inspired by the griffins of Greek mythology.

#### Marshes of Meshdad

The marshes of Meshdad, situated within the Republic of Meshdad, are a wet and biodiverse habitat.

**Principal Plants:**

* **Meshdad Reed:** Used for weaving baskets and building hut roofs.
* **Scarlet Water Lily:** An aquatic plant with red flowers, used for medicinal and decorative purposes.
* **Black Mangroves:** Trees that grow along the marshy banks, essential for preventing soil erosion.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Giant Water Serpents:** Mythic creatures said to protect the waters of the marshes. Inspired by sea serpents of African mythology.

#### Hills of Ashania

The hills of Ashania, situated within the Diarchy of Ashania, are known for their viticulture and olive cultivation.

**Principal Plants:**

* **Ashanian Grapevine:** Produces high-quality grapes for the region's fine wines.
* **Ashanian Olive:** Ancient trees producing olives for oil and food.
* **Royal Lavender:** Cultivated for its fragrance and calming properties.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Vineyard Nymphs:** Protective spirits of the vines and olives, said to guarantee abundant harvests. Inspired by Greek nymphs.

### Fauna

#### Forests of Linia

The forests of Linia harbor a diversified fauna, with numerous species of mammals, birds, and reptiles.

**Principal Animals:**

* **Linian Deer:** A large deer, hunted for its meat and hides.
* **Grey Wolf:** A pack predator, respected and feared by the local inhabitants.
* **Royal Eagle:** A majestic raptor, symbol of power and freedom.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Forest Phoenix:** A legendary bird said to be reborn from its own ashes. Inspired by the phoenix of Egyptian and Greek mythology.

#### Plains of Vallan

The plains of Vallan support a large population of herbivores and predators.

**Principal Animals:**

* **Vallanian Bison:** Large herbivores that graze on the grasslands, hunted for meat and hides.
* **Red Fox:** A cunning and adaptable animal, common across the plains.
* **Peregrine Falcon:** A fast and agile raptor, also used in falconry.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Winged Horses:** Mythic creatures said to fly, symbols of speed and freedom. Inspired by winged horses of Greek mythology.

#### Mountains of Orethia

The mountains of Orethia are inhabited by animals adapted to harsh and demanding conditions.

**Principal Animals:**

* **Iron Ibex:** A mountain animal with robust horns, living at high altitudes.
* **Mountain Lynx:** A solitary and elusive feline, skilled hunter of mountain prey.
* **Summit Eagle:** A raptor nesting on the highest peaks, symbol of power and keen sight.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Mountain Dragons:** Mythic creatures said to dwell in mountain caves, guardians of hidden treasures. Inspired by dragons of European mythology.

#### Marshes of Meshdad

The marshes of Meshdad harbor fauna adapted to wet and aquatic environments.

**Principal Animals:**

* **Marsh Crocodile:** A powerful predator living in waterways and marshes.
* **Golden Frog:** A colorful frog species, known for its distinctive call.
* **Blue Heron:** An elegant bird feeding on fish and small amphibians.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Marsh Hydra:** A mythic creature with many heads, said to watch over the waters of the marshes. Inspired by the hydra of Greek mythology.

#### Hills of Ashania

The hills of Ashania support a diversified fauna, with numerous species of mammals, birds, and insects.

**Principal Animals:**

* **Hill Goat:** An agile and hardy animal, raised for milk and meat.
* **Wild Boar:** A robust and fierce animal, hunted for meat and hides.
* **Hill Swallow:** A migratory bird, symbol of fortune and prosperity.

**Mythic Creatures:**

* **Hill Centaurs:** Half-human, half-horse creatures said to live hidden in the hills. Inspired by the centaurs of Greek mythology.

### Natural Resources

#### Minerals of the Rashian Mountains

The mountains to the northeast of Marethar are rich in precious minerals.

**Principal Resources:**

* **Gold:** Used for coins, jewelry, and decoration.
* **Silver:** Used for tools, coins, and jewelry.
* **Iron:** Essential for the construction of weapons and tools.

#### Timber of the Forests of Linia

The forests of Linia provide high-quality timber.

**Principal Resources:**

* **Cedar:** Used in construction and craftsmanship.
* **Oak:** Used to build ships and structures.
* **Pine:** Used in the making of furniture and tools.

#### Fishing in Marethar

The waters of Marethar are rich in marine resources that support the local economy.

**Principal Resources:**

* **Fish:** Species such as tuna, cod, and sardines are abundant and form an essential part of the local diet.
* **Shellfish:** Oysters, mussels, and squid are gathered along the coasts and are highly prized delicacies.
* **Seaweed:** Gathered for medicinal and culinary uses, seaweed is also used as a natural fertilizer.

### Mythic Creatures

#### Thalassia

**Sirens of Marethar:** Mythic creatures inhabiting the depths of the sea, known for their enchanting song that lures sailors toward dangerous reefs. The sirens are inspired by Greek mythology and maritime legend.

**Leviathan:** A colossal sea monster said to rise from the depths to sink ships. This creature is inspired by sea monster stories found in the legends of many Mediterranean cultures.

#### Aridonia

**Desert Ifrit:** Fire spirits inhabiting the desert dunes, known for their ability to manipulate flame and unleash sandstorms. The ifrit are inspired by Arabic and Berber mythology.

**Roc:** A gigantic predatory bird said to be able to lift elephants in its talons. The Roc is inspired by Middle Eastern and Asian legend.

#### Orethia

**Stone Golem:** Animated creatures fashioned by miners to protect the mines and mountain cities. The Golems are inspired by Hebrew folklore and European mythology.

**Fire Dragon:** A legendary creature inhabiting the mountain caves, known for its ability to breathe fire. Inspired by dragons of European and Ottoman mythologies.

#### Naharim

**Anubi Guardians:** Guardian spirits with jackal heads that protect tombs and temples, inspired by the funerary gods of Egypt. Anubis is a central figure in Egyptian mythology, associated with protection and guidance in the afterlife.

**Rainbow Serpent:** A mythic serpent that controls floods and rains, symbol of fertility and prosperity. Inspired by the Inca and Aztec myths of serpentine deities.

#### Aurelia

**Golden Griffin:** A creature with the body of a lion and the head of an eagle, symbol of power and nobility. Inspired by the griffins of Greek and Roman mythology.

**Ruin Fae:** Spirits inhabiting ancient ruins, known for their beauty and the power to protect hidden treasures. The fae are inspired by European folklore and Renaissance legend.

#### Lysandria

**Labyrinth Minotaur:** A half-human, half-bull creature inhabiting the labyrinths beneath the city-states, guarding secrets and hidden wealth. Inspired by Greek mythology, in particular the legend of the Minotaur of Crete.

**Hippocampus:** Half-horse, half-fish creatures that pull the chariots of the sea gods. Inspired by Greek and Roman tales of marine creatures.

### Flora and Fauna by Region

#### Thalassia

**Flora:**

* **Salicornia:** A succulent plant growing in coastal and marshy zones, used as food and medicine.
* **Date Palm:** Cultivated along the coasts for its nutritious and sweet fruit.

**Fauna:**

* **Royal Gull:** A common seabird along the coasts, known for its habit of following ships in search of food.
* **Marethar Sea Turtle:** A species of sea turtle that nests on the sandy beaches of Thalassia.

#### Aridonia

**Flora:**

* **Desert Acacia:** A hardy tree providing wood and gum arabic.
* **Sun Cactus:** Succulent plants that store water and flower rarely, but with vivid and colorful blooms.

**Fauna:**

* **Fennec:** A small desert fox with large ears, adapted to arid conditions.
* **Golden Scorpion:** A venomous insect common in the sandy dunes.

#### Orethia

**Flora:**

* **Iron Grass:** A robust plant growing in rock crevices, used for weaving strong rope.
* **Mountain Broom:** A shrub with yellow flowers, used to scent the air and as a medicinal plant.

**Fauna:**

* **Wild Yak:** A large bovine living at mountain elevations, hunted for meat and wool.
* **Summit Falcon:** A raptor dominating the mountain skies, also used in hunting.

#### Naharim

**Flora:**

* **Blue Poppy:** A rare flower growing along the banks of the great river, used for medicinal and ritual purposes.
* **Sacred Lotus:** An aquatic plant venerated and used in religious rituals.

**Fauna:**

* **Nilo Caiman:** A powerful reptile inhabiting the waters of the great river, feared and respected.
* **Sacred Ibis:** A symbolic bird, often depicted in hieroglyphs and temples.

#### Aurelia

**Flora:**

* **Royal Laurel:** An aromatic plant used to crown victors and for culinary purposes.
* **Blood Rose:** A deep red rose, cultivated in imperial gardens and used for medicinal purposes.

**Fauna:**

* **Imperial Wolf:** A predator living in the forests and hills, symbol of strength and unity.
* **White Dove:** A bird symbolizing peace and purity, often kept as a companion animal.

#### Lysandria

**Flora:**

* **Myrtle:** An aromatic plant used to make perfumes and in religious ceremonies.
* **Ancient Olive:** Olive trees producing high-quality oil, an integral part of the local diet.

**Fauna:**

* **Lysandrian Dolphin:** An intelligent marine mammal that often accompanies ships, considered a sign of good fortune.
* **Lysandrian Deer:** An elegant animal inhabiting the forests, hunted for meat and hides.
