## 🝖 Technology

### Metallurgy and Metalworking

**Extraction and Smelting**
- Metals in use: bronze, iron, copper, silver, lead (ritual use).
- Mining regions: [[Kingdom of Vallan|Vallan]] (iron, tin), [[Principality of Dajilia|Dajilia]] (copper and rock salt), Karnak (black copper), [[Rashian Empire|Rashia]] (deep-seam deposits).
- Techniques: bellows furnaces, modular cast molds, mixed alloys.

**Military Production**
- Vallan: segmented plate armor, ritual blades.
- Rashia: cavalry arms, ceremonial shields.
- [[Republic of Premia|Premia]]: modular legion supplies, standardized arrowheads.

**Tools and Ornaments**
- [[Aurelians]]: agricultural implements, copper cookware, liturgical objects.
- [[Thalassians]]: brass naval fittings, ritual metal clasps.
- [[Naharim]]: hammered river-metal, often engraved.

**Coinage**
- Metallic currencies struck locally; divine or civic symbols.
- [[City-State of Allasia|Allasia]] and [[Rhithian League|Rhithia]]: use of organic inlays or mixed materials.

---

### Engineering and Construction

**Urban and Monumental Architecture**
- [[Empire of Linia|Linia]], [[City-State of Allasia|Allasia]]: arches, domes, reinforced towers.
- [[Rashian Empire|Rashia]]: modular fortresses, tiered defensive walls.
- [[Diarchy of Ashania|Ashania]]: mixed stone-and-timber materials, druidic techniques.

**Civil Infrastructure**
- [[Aurelians|Aurelia]] and [[Republic of Premia|Premia]]: paved road networks, segmented bridges.
- [[Republic of Meshdad|Fahya]]: multi-level aqueducts, elevated channels.
- Dor and [[Naharim]]: stilt bridges, semi-submerged structures.

**Irrigation**
- Naharim: river rotation systems, semi-mobile dams.
- [[Aridonians|Aridonia]]: deep wells, naturally ventilated cisterns.
- [[Principality of Dajilia|Dajilia]]: ritual drainage channels, lunar flow-meters.

---

### Agriculture and Animal Husbandry

**Cultivations**
- [[Republic of Premia|Premia]], Dor: wheat, legumes, and vegetables with advanced crop rotation.
- [[Principality of Dajilia|Dajilia]]: drought-resistant grains, medicinal herbs, ritual grazing plants.
- [[Orethians|Orethia]]: controlled cultivation on terraced hillsides.

**Livestock**
- [[Aridonians|Aridonia]]: camels, desert horses.
- [[Naharim]]: water buffalo, river goats.
- [[Rashian Empire|Rashia]]: black cattle, adapted sheep.

**Medicinal Plants**
- [[Aurelians]]: pharmacological gardens, monastic greenhouses.
- [[Orethians]]: spiritual cultivations, alchemical seeds.

---

### Firearms and Artillery

**Development and Spread**
- Origin: [[Aridonians|Aridonia]] (arquebuses), exported to [[Rashian Empire|Rashia]] and [[Kingdom of Vallan|Vallan]].
- Rashia: refinement of siege artillery.
- [[Empire of Linia|Linia]]: standardization of bore and ignition.

**Weapons Technologies**
- Breech-loading cannons (limited), stone towers with movable embrasures.
- Ammunition in stone, iron, solidified sand.

**Defenses**
- Double inner walls ([[Republic of Premia|Premia]]), water bastions (Dor), mobile towers (Vallan).

---

### Navigation and Shipbuilding

**Shipbuilding**
- [[Thalassians|Thalassia]], [[Rhithian League|Rhithia]]: reinforced hulls, composite masting.
- [[Republic of Meshdad|Meshdad]]: modular vessels for variable routes.
- [[City-State of Allasia|Allasia]]: ceremonial and merchant ships, three-tier fleets.

**Nautical Instruments**
- Magnetic compasses, lunar refraction sextants, partial sea charts.
- Ritual star charts used among coastal druids.

**Maritime Trade**
- Flows: spices (from [[Republic of Meshdad|Meshdad]]), metals (from [[Rashian Empire|Rashia]]), cloth and glass (from Allasia).
- Naval protection provided by consortia, guilds, and sea cults.

---

### ⚗ Medicine and Alchemy

**Medical Practices**
- [[Aurelians]]: herbalist-physicians, civic emergency care.
- [[Orethians]]: purification rituals, symbolic transference rites.
- [[Empire of Linia|Linia]]: codified schools of medicine, regulated dissection.

**Alchemy**
- Symbolic transmutations, partial elixirs, codified potion techniques.
- Ingredients: ritual minerals, rare essences, dual elements.

**Centers of Study**
- [[Republic of Meshdad|Fahya]]: alchemical towers under censorship.
- [[Republic of Premia|Premia]]: municipal pharmacopoeias.
- [[Diarchy of Ashania|Ashania]]: oral libraries of codified formulae.

---

### 🛤 Communication and Transport

**Communications**
- Smoke and fire signals, coded drums, horse-mounted messengers.
- [[Republic of Meshdad|Fahya]]: nocturnal mirror relay system.

**Transport**
- Articulated carriages ([[Empire of Linia|Linia]]), river barges (Dor), ritual caravans ([[Principality of Dajilia|Dajilia]]).
- Suspension bridges, mountain galleries, passive tunnels between twin cities.

**Innovations**
- [[Republic of Premia|Premia]]: mobile stone inscriptions for civic records.
- [[Aridonians|Aridonia]]: ceremonial hoofguards for sacred crossings.
- [[Kingdom of Vallan|Vallan]]: rock sledges and chain systems for mountain ascent.
