## 🝖 Technology

### Metallurgy and Metalworking

**Extraction and Smelting**
- Metals in use: bronze, iron, copper, silver, lead (ritual use).
- Mining regions: Vallan (iron, tin), Dajilia (copper and rock salt), Karnak (black copper), Rashia (deep-seam deposits).
- Techniques: bellows furnaces, modular cast molds, mixed alloys.

**Military Production**
- Vallan: segmented plate armor, ritual blades.
- Rashia: cavalry arms, ceremonial shields.
- Premia: modular legion supplies, standardized arrowheads.

**Tools and Ornaments**
- Aurelians: agricultural implements, copper cookware, liturgical objects.
- Thalassians: brass naval fittings, ritual metal clasps.
- Naharim: hammered river-metal, often engraved.

**Coinage**
- Metallic currencies struck locally; divine or civic symbols.
- Allasia and Rhithia: use of organic inlays or mixed materials.

---

### Engineering and Construction

**Urban and Monumental Architecture**
- Linia, Allasia: arches, domes, reinforced towers.
- Rashia: modular fortresses, tiered defensive walls.
- Ashania: mixed stone-and-timber materials, druidic techniques.

**Civil Infrastructure**
- Aurelia and Premia: paved road networks, segmented bridges.
- Fahya: multi-level aqueducts, elevated channels.
- Dor and Naharim: stilt bridges, semi-submerged structures.

**Irrigation**
- Naharim: river rotation systems, semi-mobile dams.
- Aridonia: deep wells, naturally ventilated cisterns.
- Dajilia: ritual drainage channels, lunar flow-meters.

---

### Agriculture and Animal Husbandry

**Cultivations**
- Premia, Dor: wheat, legumes, and vegetables with advanced crop rotation.
- Dajilia: drought-resistant grains, medicinal herbs, ritual grazing plants.
- Orethia: controlled cultivation on terraced hillsides.

**Livestock**
- Aridonia: camels, desert horses.
- Naharim: water buffalo, river goats.
- Rashia: black cattle, adapted sheep.

**Medicinal Plants**
- Aurelians: pharmacological gardens, monastic greenhouses.
- Orethians: spiritual cultivations, alchemical seeds.

---

### Firearms and Artillery

**Development and Spread**
- Origin: Aridonia (arquebuses), exported to Rashia and Vallan.
- Rashia: refinement of siege artillery.
- Linia: standardization of bore and ignition.

**Weapons Technologies**
- Breech-loading cannons (limited), stone towers with movable embrasures.
- Ammunition in stone, iron, solidified sand.

**Defenses**
- Double inner walls (Premia), water bastions (Dor), mobile towers (Vallan).

---

### Navigation and Shipbuilding

**Shipbuilding**
- Thalassia, Rhithia: reinforced hulls, composite masting.
- Meshdad: modular vessels for variable routes.
- Allasia: ceremonial and merchant ships, three-tier fleets.

**Nautical Instruments**
- Magnetic compasses, lunar refraction sextants, partial sea charts.
- Ritual star charts used among coastal druids.

**Maritime Trade**
- Flows: spices (from Meshdad), metals (from Rashia), cloth and glass (from Allasia).
- Naval protection provided by consortia, guilds, and sea cults.

---

### ⚗ Medicine and Alchemy

**Medical Practices**
- Aurelians: herbalist-physicians, civic emergency care.
- Orethians: purification rituals, symbolic transference rites.
- Linia: codified schools of medicine, regulated dissection.

**Alchemy**
- Symbolic transmutations, partial elixirs, codified potion techniques.
- Ingredients: ritual minerals, rare essences, dual elements.

**Centers of Study**
- Fahya: alchemical towers under censorship.
- Premia: municipal pharmacopoeias.
- Ashania: oral libraries of codified formulae.

---

### 🛤 Communication and Transport

**Communications**
- Smoke and fire signals, coded drums, horse-mounted messengers.
- Fahya: nocturnal mirror relay system.

**Transport**
- Articulated carriages (Linia), river barges (Dor), ritual caravans (Dajilia).
- Suspension bridges, mountain galleries, passive tunnels between twin cities.

**Innovations**
- Premia: mobile stone inscriptions for civic records.
- Aridonia: ceremonial hoofguards for sacred crossings.
- Vallan: rock sledges and chain systems for mountain ascent.
