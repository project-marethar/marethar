# The Book of the Second Life

**Origin**: Fahya
**Function**: Ritual tome. Said to contain the spell for bringing exactly one person back from the dead.
**Rumours**: No one knows the cost. Some say it brings back only the body; others say it steals life from whoever reads it. It has been sealed with red wax, and the wax does not melt.
**Status**: Held in a forbidden library. No one knows which one.
