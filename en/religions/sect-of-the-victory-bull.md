# Sect of the Victory Bull

**Type:** Cult
**Form:** Cult
**Culture:** Thalassian
**Area:** Thalassia
**Faithful:** 12,438

**Description:** The Sect of the Victory Bull venerates Adrarad, the Creator. This cult is known for its secret rites and ceremonies celebrating the strength and power of the buffalo as a symbol of victory. The faithful believe that Adrarad grants strength and success in battle and in the challenges of life.

**Origins and Religious Practices:** The cult originates among the warrior tribes of Thalassia, who considered the buffalo a sacred animal and symbol of power. Rituals include tribal dances, offerings of meat, and initiation ceremonies for young warriors. Ceremonies take place in sacred sites, often deep within forests or in hidden caves.

**Clergy Structure:** The sect is led by shamans and tribal chiefs who hold knowledge of the secret rites and conduct the ceremonies. They are also the keepers of the cult's legends and sacred stories.
